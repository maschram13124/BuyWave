About BuyWave

Welcome to BuyWave, a cutting-edge stock analysis platform built for traders and investors who want a smarter way to analyze market trends.

Who We Are

We are a team of five students from the MIS4100 class, passionate about technology, finance, and data-driven decision-making. This project is our semester-long effort to build a **powerful yet user-friendly** tool that helps users navigate the stock market with confidence.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide **real-time stock data, historical trends, and AI-driven insights** to help investors make better decisions. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced trader, BuyWave aims to give you the tools needed to stay ahead in the market.
